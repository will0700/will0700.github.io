About Me

I am a full stack web developer currently living in Los Angeles, California. I started developing in earnest when I attended and completed the Coding Dojo bootcamp program in 2019 to add Django/Python, Spring/Java, and MEAN stacks to my toolbox. My focus is on expanding my knowledge of architecture and back-end server side logic, although I maintain proficiency in front-end technologies and libraries for full stack capability. My prior professional background is in accountancy with a bachelor's degree in Economics from UC Berkeley. Outside of programming, I enjoy snowboarding and surfing as the seasons cycle and slowly teaching myself the bass guitar.
